About DJ Muscle

Mandla P. Reissmann

"I'm not a musician, but I play music." DJ Muscle is a versatile open format DJ from Mbabane, Eswatini, with over a decade of experience in the music industry. Known as 'The All Rounder' for his seamless mixing across multiple genres, he has performed at major festivals and continues to captivate audiences internationally.

Currently based between Eswatini and Cork, Ireland, DJ Muscle is bringing the best of African rhythms and global beats to dance floors across Europe. With 111 shows on Mixcloud and performances at iconic venues like Cyprus Avenue, Crane Lane Theatre, and House On Fire Amphitheatre, his unique blend of Afro House, Amapiano, and everything groovy keeps the energy high.
